About

Learn more about me

My name is Roberto. I'm passionate about programming, computer security and hacking. I work as a computer developer and in my spare time I enjoy programming, discovering and experimenting. The technologies I work with most frequently are: Java, Spring Boot, Angular, BASH, Docker and Android. I love Linux systems, I like MacOs and I use Windows to play Call Of Duty and World of Warcraft!

This is my site, you will find some resources that may be useful to you, I like to share my knowledge. I'm not a graduate, I learned a lot of my knowledge on the web thanks to sites like StackOverflow, GitHub, Wikipedia and other blogs of people like me (who want to share without any price). I see on the internet an immense source of knowledge, one of the most complex human creations that gives us an enormous source of FREE KNOWLEDGE.
